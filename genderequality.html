GENDER EQUALITY

Information

It is a Gender Equality Model that is part of the “Gender Equality Certification” program of the National Women's Institute, whose objective is to “develop, promote and promote gender equality in organizations… It seeks to institutionalize gender equality policies. gender equity and promote equal opportunities between men and women in access to employment, working conditions, as well as professional development, training and participation in decision-making processes.”
Opinion 

my opinion

I think the idea and obviously, the goal are excellent and the intention is good, but I think the goal has a perspective of a high country such as some parts of Europe where they have and advanced gender equality. But in places where the rights of women are still in debate such as Mexico and other south America countries, a couple of years are not enough for a full transformation.
The transformation of gender equality is not only from the legal and political part, is also from the society in a society that is difficult to talk about gender equality or the problems that all women had to face day through day is still a lot of work to do. The goal would be ok if they considered not only the legal part they could consider the part of society, society has a lot of weight in this themes.
